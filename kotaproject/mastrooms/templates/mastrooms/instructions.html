    Instructions :

    Please go through the Instructions before sending us detail of your Property. Given below is the detail of the Fields, you have to send us in order to register your property. You can send us the detail in writing through whatsapp chat (Click on the Button below to send details on Whatsapp ) or Email us at mastrooms@gmail.com. You can also send by writing all the details of your Property on a Paper and send us the photo of the paper.
    All fields with asterisk ( * ) are compulsary. By sending details, we assume that you have read our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.


    * Name of your Property :

    It is the name of your Building. Example can be : XYZ Boys'/Girls' Hostels / PG , XYZ Sadan / Villa / Nivas etc.

    * Address of your Property :

    Provide House no/ Plot Number/ Street with Name of the Colony.Example can be : 1011A, XYZ Nagar Ist, XYZ Colony etc.

    Landmark :

    Famous places near to your Property It will help the Tenants(students) to locate your Property easily. Example can be : Near Allen Sankalp Building or Near PMC Hospital or Near City Mall etc.

    * Contact number :

    Provide the contact number, email address (if possible) so that Tenants(students) can contact you directly. If your Hostel have Wardens, then you can give the contact information of your Warden also.(There is no Limit on the contact numbers provided by you, but remember that all the contact detail will be visible at our website.)

    * Category :

    Boys'/ Girls Hostel / PG, Family rooms, Flats etc.

    Occupancy:

    Single / Double seater. This field is optional. If you will not provide field, we will assume it as Single Seater.

    Amenities:

    Amenities are the accessories or belonging to your room. Fill this field precisely as more Amenities means more Tenants(students).Example can be : A.C, Attached Bathroom, Power Backup, Cooler, Water Purifier, Water Cooler, Geyser, Mattress, Pillow, Curtains, Bucket etc.

    Description:

    Tell something about your Rooms. It may be the Cleanliness, Locality around property, Nearby shops etc. Example can be : Room is airy and has an emple space of 15ft X 15ft. Room is at a walking distance from Allen Safalya Building. Room is designed in such a way, that no noise can reach you when you are studying. Our Building has Mess facility with good quality food. Alos, we ran Daily need shop, so that your daily needs are Fullfilled. Remember to describe your Building nicely.
